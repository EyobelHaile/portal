About Me

Hello! My name is Bella Haile, and I grew up in Asmara, Eritrea. I'm a Year Up student pursuing a track in software development and support. At an early age, I developed a passion for engineering and technology and loved problem-solving in creative ways.

I had the chance to work as a design assurance engineer intern at Boston Scientific last summer, where I put my analytical thinking and problem-solving abilities into practice to implement product design changes, spot quality problems, and keep documentation up to date.

After this experience, I decided to continue working in quality as a software engineer and therefore joined the Year Up program to help me get there. I also have experience designing circuits and products using electrical tools like transformers, multimeters, and resistors.

My primary focus is to develop my coding abilities to get an entryway to the tech industry and gain experience to advance my career. I'm learning Python, HTML, JavaScript, and CSS to increase my coding and software knowledge.

I like to swim and spend time with my family in my spare time. I take pride in knowing my family is important to me. I recently began crocheting and have been enjoying the process. Using YouTube as a resource, I teach myself to prepare various cultural dishes. My family thinks I have reached the chef level.

Last, but not least, I am an effective leader who is always eager to lend a helping hand.
