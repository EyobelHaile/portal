About Me

I'm Bella Haile, born and raised in Asmara, Eritrea. Currently, I am a student at Year Up pursuing software development and support track. At an early age, I developed a passion for engineering and technology and loved problem solving in creative ways.

Last summer, I had an opportunity interning as a design assurance engineer at a medical device manufacturing company where I was able to use my critical thinking and problem-solving skills to implement product design changes, identify quality issues, and maintain documentation.

After this experience, I decided to continue working in quality as a software engineer and therefore joined the Year Up program to help me get there. Additionally, I have experience working with electrical equipment such as transformers, multimeter and resistors to design circuits and products. Currently, I am learning python, HTML, JavaScript, and CSS to expand my knowledge of coding and software.

My main goal right now is to improve my coding skills so that I can get an entry way into the tech industry to gain experiences and further my career. Lastly, I am an empathetic and effective leader who is always willing to lend a helping hand when needed.
